Who's Sylvie and Why This Program?

This course was inspired by the challenges I faced as a young woman who was desperate to escape the scorns of a patriarchal society that makes it hard for a dependent woman to truly enjoy the beauties of life.

At the age of 16, I lost my father to a ghastly motor accident. In 2009, I went into the corporate world and always had a side business to complement my income and responsibilities.

I hold a diploma in banking and Finance, A BSc in Accounting, finance and Audit, An MBA in Global business and I also have 12 years of corporate work experience across different industries.

In 2017, I resigned from my job as finance manager for a multinational company and took time off to obtain my MBA in global business. During that time, I worked part time whilst earning some extra income on the side.

When covid 19 hit and the lockdowns were imposed, I went from a state of confusion and depression from being confined with 2 small children, to creating over 5 different sources of income.

My journey to achieving becoming Ms independent who enjoys financial freedom and a balanced life, although bumpy, has been incredibly rewarding.

Getting out of my own head and overcoming my limiting beliefs led me to the place where I have always wanted to be — a happy mother who spends plenty of time with her kids, offering as much love as I could possibly give to my partner, whilst living a purposeful and financially free life.

Between November 2020 and December 2022, I have transformed my $2500 seed capital into just over $ 120,000 from home and mostly using my mobile phone. And the cherry on the cake is being a happier mum and partner.

I now have more than 5 sources of income and I am continuously building on that.

I wholeheartedly believe that with your participation and action, you will start to experience success as a business owner within a short span of time.

Course Outline

How The Program is Laid Out

Each session will consist of a Live Call, Teachings and Q&As - One hour a week live call for a face-to-face interactive coaching experience.

Week 1 – Clarity

• Reinforcing the success philosophy
• Discussing where you are, where you want to be
• Discover the Clarity Tool to creating your new life
• Practical Mind stretch exercise (very powerful exercise to break limiting beliefs)
• Creating your 12 months achievable vision board
• Homework (what you’ll be expected to do before next meeting)

Week 2– Brain Storming

• Resource Audit (determining everything you have that would help your new business)
• Talent Management (organise your human skills to match your business idea)
• 7 Levels Narrow (Discover your why to self-motivate, accomplish your goals and live a purposeful life)
• Homework (what you’ll be expected to do before next meeting)

Week 3 – Business Development

• A business idea (what you’ll be expected to do before next meeting)
• The Critical success Factors (CSFs) (determine all the necessaries for a success business idea to take off)
• The Breakdown(determine skills, people, certificates your business idea needs)
• Homework (what you’ll be expected to do before next meeting)

Week 4 – Setting The Baseline

• Customer avatar (the profile of the perfect customer for your business)
• Market Research (techniques to gather information on your perfect customer)
• Business plan (a written document that describes your business)
• Homework (what you’ll be expected to do before next meeting)

Week 5 – Business Development

• Branding (Create a distinct identity for your business in the mind of your perfect customer)
• Social media handles (creating your unique usernames for each social channel)
• The Distractions (acknowledging & dealing with mental, physical, and emotional blocks that would get in your way)
• Launching Preps (going through a check list in preparation to making your business public)
• Momentum builder (how to maintain consistency and persistency)
• Other discussions (Feedback on the program and anywhere else you may need support
